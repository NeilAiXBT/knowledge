### 1. AI Model Development: MECE-Compliant Stages and Logical Classification  
#### 1.1 Overview and Numbered Stages (English/Chinese)

   This stage involves collecting, exploring, cleaning, and augmenting datasets to ensure data quality and relevance for AI model training.

   This phase embeds the AI model within a larger software ecosystem, ensuring compatibility, scalability, documentation, and user interface creation.  
   软件开发与集成阶段将AI模型嵌入更大的软件系统，确保兼容性、可扩展性、文档完善及用户界面开发。

   The AI model is released into production environments for real-time or batch inference, with ongoing monitoring, logging, retraining, and maintenance.  
   部署与系统运维阶段包括将模型上线至生产环境，进行实时或批量推理，并持续监控、日志记录和再训练以及维护。

   治理与伦理监督阶段涵盖整个开发周期中的法规遵从、隐私保护、偏见缓解及负责任的AI开发实践。
